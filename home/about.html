About Us

Our Mission

Founded in 1999, Mocha Moves was created with the aim to promote and celebrate hip hop and urban dance culture at MIT and throughout the Greater Boston Area.

Dance is our passion and we love being able to share our unique Mocha Flava with others!

Events

Each Fall, Mocha Moves hosts an urban dance competition called Ring The Alarm on MIT's campus. Teams come from all around the Boston dance scene - MIT, other local colleges, and outside teams - to compete in this annual charity event.

Each Spring, Mocha Moves puts on a full length (approx. 2 hour long) dance show called the Mocha Show. This show consists of sets entirely made up of dancers from Mocha Moves. Members work over IAP and February to choreograph, teach, stage, and perform this full-length show. Every Mocha Show also has a "Classic" piece from Ring The Alarm two years prior.
