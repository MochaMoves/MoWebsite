Established in 1999, Mocha Moves is an MIT-based dance team whose mission is to be a creative outlet for members to develop their skills. We share our talent and passion for dance through hosting performances, such as Ring the Alarm and Mocha Show, and participating in many others hosted throughout the Greater Boston Area. We strive to learn and grow together by cultivating our talents and connections with the Boston dance community. On Mocha, the grind never stops!
